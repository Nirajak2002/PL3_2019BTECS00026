    Success Quotes

    Success is the sum of small efforts - repeated day in and day out.

           - Robert Collier

    Success isn't handed to anyone. It's achieved by hard work and what you do every day to get to your goal.
    Your efforts don't have to be big leaps and bounds to achieve success, take a realistic approach to taking the necessary steps.
       

    Life Quotes

    Life isn't about finding yourself. Life is about creating yourself.

           - George Bernard Shaw

    isn't something you just find and that allowing your life to be decided by something you found during the course of it isn't enough. ...
    It is based on the opportunities we create throughout our life.
       

    Motivational Quotes

    Aim for the Moon , if you miss you may hit a star.

           - W. Clement Stone

    This means have an ambitious goal and go for it. If you miss the goal, you'll still have achieved something.
